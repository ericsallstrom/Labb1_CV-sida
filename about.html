About me

I'm Eric Sällström, a software developer residing in Dalarna, Sweden. Currently pursuing my studies in 'System Development .NET' at Edugrade. Other than my role as a dedicated and prospective developer, I find joy in activities like snowboarding and cross country skiing as well as spending time with family and friends.


As a developer, I'm inquisitive, meticulous, and ambitious. I thrive on challenges, constantly seeking opportunities for learning and growth within the ever-evolving world of technology.


Beyond the realm of System Development, my passion lies with music, particulary in the facets of music production and mixing. In my spare time, I contribute to the operation of running a recording studio alongside my family. This venture not only adds a another creative dimension to my life but also marks the point where my love for technology seamlessly converges with the world of music.
